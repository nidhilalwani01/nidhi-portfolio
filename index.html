About Me

Designing with empathy,
driven by curiosity

I'm Nidhi Lalwani, a UI/UX and product designer with a passion for solving complex problems through clean, user-centered design. I believe great products start with deeply understanding the people who use them.

My background spans health tech, sustainability, and civic technology — areas where thoughtful design can make a meaningful real-world impact. I love collaborating across disciplines to turn research into elegant, functional interfaces.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring design systems, reading up on cognitive psychology, or sketching ideas in my notebook.
