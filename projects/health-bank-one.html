UX Case Study  ·  2025

Health Bank One

A women-centered healthcare UX case study using mixed-methods research, Figma prototyping, and usability testing to improve trust and clarity.

  • UX Research
  • Wireframing
  • Figma Prototyping
  • Usability Testing

Overview

Health Bank One set out to simplify the way women navigate healthcare benefits and make decisions about their coverage. Through a series of user interviews and competitive audits, the research surfaced a consistent pain point: existing interfaces placed too much cognitive load on users at moments of high stress.

The design response prioritised plain language, clear information hierarchy, and reassuring micro-copy, validated through two rounds of moderated usability testing in Figma.

My Role

End-to-end UX: research planning, interview facilitation, affinity mapping, wireframes, high-fidelity prototype in Figma, and usability test analysis.
