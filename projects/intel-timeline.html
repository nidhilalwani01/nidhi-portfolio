Intel Sustainability Timeline Webpage

An interactive, narrative-driven timeline webpage that chronicles Intel's sustainability journey — bringing decades of environmental commitments to life through compelling storytelling and data visualization.

Role

UX Design Intern

Duration

3 months (2023)

Tools

Figma, HTML/CSS, JavaScript

Type

Web (Desktop + Mobile)

Intel Sustainability Timeline — Cover Image

Overview

As part of Intel's broader sustainability communication strategy, I was tasked with designing an interactive timeline webpage that would allow visitors to explore Intel's environmental milestones from the 1990s through present day. The goal was to make complex sustainability data accessible, engaging, and emotionally resonant for both general audiences and stakeholders.

Problem

Intel had a wealth of sustainability data and achievements but no cohesive way to present them to the public. Existing reports were dense PDFs, and the sustainability section of the corporate website lacked engagement. Users weren't staying long enough to understand the depth of Intel's environmental work.

  • Average time on sustainability pages: under 90 seconds
  • Data was scattered across multiple reports and sub-pages
  • No visual storytelling — raw numbers without narrative context
  • Not optimized for mobile, where 60% of visitors arrived
Current State Analysis / Audit

Goals

  • Create an engaging, scroll-driven narrative experience
  • Surface key milestones in a visually compelling timeline format
  • Ensure full responsiveness across desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Keep performance high — fast load, accessible, SEO-friendly
  • Align with Intel's brand guidelines and sustainability visual language

Process

I began with a competitive audit of sustainability timelines from companies like Apple, Microsoft, and Patagonia. This informed our understanding of effective patterns — and where Intel could differentiate.

Competitive Audit

Content strategy: Worked closely with Intel's corporate sustainability team to identify the 20 most impactful milestones. Wrote plain-language summaries to make technical achievements accessible.

Information Architecture: Organized milestones into thematic categories (Energy, Water, Waste, Supply Chain) with chronological structure. Created a filtering system for users to focus on areas of interest.

Wireframes & Information Architecture

Visual Design: Developed a warm, earthy color palette inspired by environmental themes while staying true to Intel's brand. Used custom icons and micro-animations to reinforce the narrative.

Visual Design & High-Fidelity Mockups

Prototyping & Handoff: Built a fully interactive Figma prototype before working alongside the front-end team for implementation. Contributed HTML/CSS during development to ensure design fidelity.

Outcome

  • Average time on page increased from 90 seconds to 4.5 minutes
  • Mobile bounce rate decreased by 33%
  • Featured in Intel's 2023 Corporate Responsibility Report
  • Received internal design award for "Best Sustainability Communication"

Reflection

This project reinforced that data storytelling is as much about what you leave out as what you include. Choosing the right 20 milestones from hundreds of achievements was more challenging than designing the page itself. Clarity always beats completeness.
